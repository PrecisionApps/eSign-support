Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how eSign ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App. This policy applies to the App and related services provided by Kaan Yildiz ("we," "us," or "our").

If you have questions, contact us at: support@esign-app.com

1. Summary

  • ✕
    No tracking: We do not track you across apps or websites and we do not use App Tracking Transparency because we do not perform tracking.
  • ✕
    No ads / no sale: We do not sell personal data and we do not share data for advertising purposes.
  • ⚙
    AI processing: When you use AI features (e.g., smart field detection or document analysis), document content may be transmitted to OpenAI to generate responses.
  • ◉
    Document data is user-initiated: Documents and signature data are processed only when you explicitly initiate a signing or AI-assisted action within the App.

2. Information We Collect

A) Information you provide

  • Documents: PDF files and other documents you import, upload, or create within the App for the purpose of signing or sending.
  • Signature data: Your drawn or typed signature as captured within the App when you sign a document.
  • Form field inputs: Text, dates, initials, or other values you enter into document fields.
  • Signer information: Names and email addresses you enter when preparing a document for others to sign.

B) AI-assisted features (only when you use them)

If you use AI-powered features (such as smart field detection or document summarization), the App may send relevant document content to OpenAI to generate results. This occurs only when you explicitly trigger an AI feature.

Important: AI features are optional. The App does not send document content to any external service unless you explicitly trigger an AI-assisted action.

C) Technical information (minimal)

We may process minimal technical data needed to operate the App, such as app version, device language, and basic error information. We do not use this information for tracking or advertising.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only to provide and operate the App, including to:

  • Enable you to sign, send, and manage documents
  • Generate AI-assisted results you request (e.g., smart field detection, document analysis) when you trigger them
  • Maintain reliability, prevent abuse, and debug failures
  • Provide customer support if you contact us

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, data brokerage, or tracking.

4. Third-Party AI Processing (OpenAI)

To provide AI-powered features, the App may transmit certain document content off-device to OpenAI.

A) Who we share with

  • OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C.) — the sole AI provider used by eSign.

B) What data is sent

Depending on which AI features you use, the App may send:

  • Relevant portions of document text needed to perform the AI-assisted action (e.g., field detection, document summarization)
  • The minimum necessary context to generate an accurate result
  • Minimal technical metadata required to route and deliver the request (e.g., app version, device language)

C) When data is sent

  • AI-assisted features: Only when you explicitly trigger an AI feature within the App (e.g., tapping "Detect Fields" or requesting a document summary).
  • Document content is never sent to OpenAI passively or in the background.

D) User permission and controls

  • AI features require explicit user action — they are never triggered automatically.
  • You can choose not to use any AI features; all core signing functionality works without them.

E) Data retention by OpenAI

We do not sell your document data, and we do not allow OpenAI to use it for advertising through our App. However, OpenAI may process and retain request data according to their own terms and policies for purposes such as safety, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

If you do not want document content processed by OpenAI, do not use AI-assisted features. All core signing, sending, and document management features work fully without AI.

5. What We Do Not Collect or Use

  • We do not track your activity across other companies' apps and websites.
  • We do not collect or share data for targeted advertising.
  • We do not sell personal data or document content.
  • We do not store your documents on our servers — documents remain on your device unless you explicitly share them.
  • We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data beyond what you choose to include in documents you process.

6. Sharing of Information

We share information only in these cases:

  • With OpenAI to fulfill AI-assisted requests you explicitly trigger, as described above
  • With recipients you designate when you send a document for signing (e.g., sharing a document link or file)
  • With service providers strictly for app operation (if applicable), under confidentiality obligations
  • If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security
  • In a business transfer (e.g., merger/acquisition), with notice where required

7. Data Storage and Deletion

  • Documents, signatures, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • You can delete individual documents or all app data within the App at any time.
  • Deleting the App from your device will remove all locally stored documents and data.
  • Documents you have sent to others for signing may remain accessible to those recipients until they delete them.
Note: Content sent to OpenAI via AI features may be handled according to OpenAI's retention policies (see Section 4E).

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live, including where our service providers and AI providers operate. These locations may have different data protection laws.

11. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your information. You can contact us at support@esign-app.com.

You can delete your documents and data directly within the App at any time, and you can choose not to use AI-assisted features entirely.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Effective Date" above and, when appropriate, provide additional notice in the App.
